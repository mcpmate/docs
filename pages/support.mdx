- **Response Time**: Usually within 24 hours on weekdays

### Community Resources

## Reporting Issues

When reporting problems, please include:
- **MCPMate version** (found in Settings → About)
- **macOS version** and hardware (Intel/Apple Silicon)
- **Steps to reproduce** the issue
- **Error messages** from the logs panel
- **Screenshots** if relevant

## Feature Requests

Have an idea for MCPMate? We'd love to hear it!

- Vote on existing feature requests

## About MCPMate

- **Version**: 1.0.0 (check Settings for current version)
- **Platform**: macOS (Intel & Apple Silicon)
- **Pricing**: Core features free for individuals, advanced features require subscription
- **Enterprise**: Enhanced monitoring, team management, and deployment tools available
